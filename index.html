Characters

Elsa

Elsa is the perfect mythic character – magical and larger than life.

Anna

Anna is the perfect fairytale character; unflappable, she is the forever optimist.

Kristoff

Kristoff is a true outdoorsman. He lives high up in the mountains where he harvests ice and sells it to the kingdom of Arendelle

Olaf

He's Olaf and he likes warm hugs. He is by far the friendliest snowman to walk the mountains above Arendelle.

Sven

A reindeer with the heart of a Labrador, Sven is Kristoff's loyal friend, sleigh-puller and conscience.

Snowgies

Snowgies are little snowmen Elsa unwittingly creates every time she sneezes—and she sneezes a lot.

Hans

Hans is a handsome royal from a neighboring kingdom who comes to Arendelle for Elsa's coronation.

Duke Of Weselton

What the Duke of Weselton lacks in stature, he makes up for in arrogance and showboating.

Marshmallow

Marshmallow is an enormous icy snowman born from Elsa's powers.

Oaken

Oaken runs Wandering Oaken's Trading Post and Sauna.

Trolls

The trolls are a group of creatures who reside in the Valley of the Living Rock. They are very hospitable and towards their visitors.The trolls also love to set up romantic relationships,to the point that Kristoff referred to them as "love experts".
